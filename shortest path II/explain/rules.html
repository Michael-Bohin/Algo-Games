Your goal is to find the cheapest path out of 6 possible different combinations and calculate its total price. There are two different views – satelite map and graph of flights. You can toggle between them by clicking on buttons or using hotkeys ‘s’ and ‘f’.

To view different paths separately you can either click on stars representing the city that the path contains or use hotkeys on numpad 1 through 6. Hotkey ‘0’ resets the view to display all paths, but without their distances. To view distances only from one city you can hover with mouse over its star.

Once you have the answer fill it in the graph of flights and click on check answer.

Good luck!
