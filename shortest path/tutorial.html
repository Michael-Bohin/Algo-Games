Tutorial

You will be given twelve possible flight paths that will get you from city A to city B.

Your goal will be to find the path with shortest total distance.

Each path consists of 4 flights.

On the satelite image you can see the city you are in and the city you would like to get to connected by red line. Indicating that the direct flight is missing.

Calculations memory tab will be your best friend. In order to see the individual paths, click on the names of their last cities. After you have added the numbers, you can use the fields to the right as your memory. Or write it down on piece of paper if you like. :)

By the way, you can compare the length of the paths visually and than only count manually the top candidates for shortest path.
